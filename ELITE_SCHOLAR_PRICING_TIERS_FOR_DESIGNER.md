## 📊 Pricing Psychology

### Recommended Pricing Structure:

```
STANDARD:  ₦X/month    (Base price)
PREMIUM:   ₦2X/month   (2x Standard - "Best Value")
ELITE:     ₦4X/month   (4x Standard - "Complete Solution")
```

**Why this works:**
- Premium appears as "best value" (most features per naira)
- Elite is positioned as "investment" not "expense"
- Standard is "entry point" to get schools onboarded
