### ❌ Missing for 100% GAAP Compliance:

## HIGH PRIORITY (Must Implement)

### 1. Accrual vs Cash Basis Accounting
**Gap**: Currently only tracks when fees are charged, not payment status
**GAAP Requirement**: Track both Accounts Receivable and Cash separately
**Impact**: Critical for financial statement accuracy

### 2. Bad Debt Allowance
**Gap**: No provision for uncollectible student fees
**GAAP Requirement**: Estimate and record allowance for doubtful accounts
**Impact**: Required for realistic financial reporting

### 3. Deferred Revenue (Prepayments)
**Gap**: No handling of advance payments
**GAAP Requirement**: Record prepayments as liability until service provided
**Impact**: Essential for multi-term fee payments

### 4. Period-End Adjusting Entries
**Gap**: No automatic period-end adjustments
**GAAP Requirement**: Accrue expenses, recognize deferred revenue
**Impact**: Required for accurate period reporting

## MEDIUM PRIORITY

### 5. Financial Statement Generation
**Gap**: No GAAP-compliant financial statements
**GAAP Requirement**: Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow
**Impact**: Needed for audits and compliance

### 6. Inventory Valuation (for Items)
**Gap**: No proper inventory accounting methods
**GAAP Requirement**: FIFO, LIFO, or Weighted Average costing
**Impact**: Required for accurate cost of goods sold

### 7. Multi-Period Revenue Recognition
### 8. Related Party Transactions
### 9. Contingent Liabilities
### 10. Segment Reporting
