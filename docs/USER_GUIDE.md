## 🎯 **Core Features**

### **1. Nigerian School Templates** ⚡
**What it does**: Creates complete timetables based on Nigerian education standards

**How to use**:
1. Click "Nigerian Templates" button
2. Choose template:
   - **Primary**: 8:00 AM - 2:30 PM (7 periods)
   - **Secondary**: 7:30 AM - 3:00 PM (8 periods)
3. Click "Generate" → Done!

**Result**: Complete weekly schedule with breaks, lunch, and assembly

### **2. AI Optimization** 🤖
**What it does**: Automatically optimizes your timetable for better learning

**Features**:
- **Morning STEM**: Math/Science scheduled in morning (better focus)
- **Teacher Balance**: No teacher gets overloaded
- **Conflict Prevention**: No double-booking of teachers/rooms

**How to use**:
1. After generating template, click "AI Optimize"
2. System analyzes 45+ teacher assignments
3. Automatically improves schedule
4. Shows optimization score

### **3. Cultural Integration** 🕌
**What it does**: Respects Islamic practices and Nigerian culture

**Features**:
- **Prayer Times**: Automatic Dhuhr (12:20) and Asr (15:45) breaks
- **Jummah Friday**: Special 45-minute Friday prayer (12:15)
- **Ramadan Mode**: Modified schedule (8:30 AM - 1:30 PM)
- **Nigerian Holidays**: Automatic accommodation

**How to use**:
- Prayer times are automatically included
- Toggle Ramadan mode in settings
- System handles cultural requirements

## 🔧 **Troubleshooting**

### **Common Issues**:

**Q: "No templates showing"**
A: Check internet connection, refresh page

**Q: "AI optimization not working"**  
A: Ensure teachers are assigned to subjects first

**Q: "Prayer times wrong"**
A: Times are set for Lagos timezone (GMT+1)

**Q: "Can't generate timetable"**
A: Select a section first, then try again
